### Grant Types That May Apply to Your Business:

#### 1. **Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) Program**
- **Agency:** Various (NSF, NIH, DOD, etc.)
- **Focus:** Technology innovation, software development
- **Relevance:** If you develop proprietary digital marketing tools or platforms
- **Search Terms:** "SBIR", "Small Business Innovation Research", "Technology Innovation"

#### 2. **Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR)**
- **Agency:** Various federal agencies
- **Focus:** Collaborative research with universities/research institutions
- **Relevance:** If partnering with universities on digital marketing research

#### 3. **Economic Development Grants**
- **Agency:** Department of Commerce, Economic Development Administration
- **Focus:** Business development, job creation, regional economic growth
- **Relevance:** Your business supports local economic development in Greensburg/Westmoreland County
- **Search Terms:** "Economic Development", "Small Business Development", "Regional Development"

#### 4. **Rural Business Development Grants**
- **Agency:** USDA Rural Development
- **Focus:** Business development in rural areas
- **Relevance:** If Greensburg/Westmoreland County qualifies as rural
- **Search Terms:** "Rural Business", "USDA Business Development"

#### 5. **State-Level Grants (Pennsylvania)**
- **Agency:** Pennsylvania Department of Community and Economic Development (DCED)

#### 6. **Workforce Development Grants**
- **Agency:** Department of Labor
- **Focus:** Training programs, job creation
- **Relevance:** If you provide training or create jobs
- **Search Terms:** "Workforce Development", "Job Training", "Employment"

#### 7. **Cybersecurity and Technology Grants**
- **Agency:** Various (DHS, NIST)

- **Search Terms:** "Cybersecurity", "Digital Infrastructure", "Technology Security"

   - **Category:** Business and Commerce, Science and Technology, Regional Development
   - **Agency:** Filter by specific agencies
   - **Keyword:** Try "small business", "technology", "economic development", "digital"

## Step 3: Qualifying for Grants

### Common Eligibility Requirements:

#### For Small Business Grants:
- ✅ Registered business entity (LLC, Corporation, etc.)
- ✅ Small business size standards (typically <500 employees)
- ✅ Business location (some grants are location-specific)
- ✅ Business age (some require minimum years in operation)
- ✅ Financial stability
- ✅ Ability to match funds (some grants require matching)

#### Strengths for Your Business:
- ✅ Small business serving local community
- ✅ Technology/digital services focus
- ✅ Job creation potential
- ✅ Economic development impact in Westmoreland County

## Step 4: Application Process

### Step-by-Step Application:

1. **Review the Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO)**
   - Read the entire document carefully
   - Note all deadlines (submit early!)
   - Understand evaluation criteria
   - Check required forms and attachments

2. **Create a Workspace in Grants.gov**
   - Log into Grants.gov
   - Find your grant opportunity
   - Click "Create Workspace"
   - Invite team members if needed

3. **Complete Required Forms**
   - **SF-424:** Application for Federal Assistance (standard form)
   - **SF-424A:** Budget Information
   - **SF-424B:** Assurances
   - **Project Narrative:** Your detailed proposal
   - **Budget Narrative:** Explanation of costs
   - **Other agency-specific forms**

4. **Write a Strong Project Narrative**
   - **Problem Statement:** What problem does your project solve?
   - **Objectives:** Clear, measurable goals
   - **Methodology:** How you'll achieve objectives
   - **Timeline:** Detailed project schedule
   - **Expected Outcomes:** Measurable results
   - **Sustainability:** How project continues after grant

5. **Prepare Budget**
   - Personnel costs
   - Equipment/supplies
   - Travel (if applicable)
   - Indirect costs
   - Matching funds (if required)

6. **Review and Submit**
   - Double-check all forms
   - Verify all attachments included
   - Submit at least 2-3 days before deadline
   - Get confirmation email

7. **Track Application**
   - Monitor status in Grants.gov
   - Respond to any requests for additional information
   - Wait for award notification

## Step 5: Tips for Success

### Writing a Winning Grant Proposal:

1. **Follow Instructions Exactly**
   - Use required font and formatting
   - Stay within page limits
   - Include all required sections
   - Meet all deadlines

2. **Tell a Compelling Story**
   - Connect your project to grant objectives
   - Show community impact
   - Demonstrate need
   - Highlight your unique qualifications

3. **Be Specific and Measurable**
   - Use concrete numbers and metrics
   - Define success clearly
   - Include realistic timelines
   - Show how you'll measure outcomes

4. **Demonstrate Capacity**
   - Show past success
   - Highlight team expertise
   - Provide client testimonials
   - Show financial stability

5. **Show Sustainability**
   - Explain how project continues after grant
   - Show revenue model
   - Demonstrate long-term viability

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- ❌ Missing deadlines
- ❌ Not following formatting requirements
- ❌ Vague or unclear proposals
- ❌ Unrealistic budgets
- ❌ Not addressing evaluation criteria
- ❌ Incomplete applications
