Frequently Asked Questions About SEO

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically begin appearing within 3-6 months. However, some improvements like technical SEO fixes and local optimization can show results sooner. We'll provide monthly reports tracking your progress, including keyword rankings, organic traffic, and conversion improvements.

What's the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?

Local SEO focuses on optimizing your online presence to appear in local search results when people search for services in your area (like "web design Greensburg PA"). Regular SEO targets broader, national keywords. For most Greensburg businesses, local SEO is more effective because it targets customers who are actually looking for services in your area.

Do I need to be on the first page of Google?

While being on the first page is ideal, ranking in the top 3 positions drives the most traffic. Studies show that the first three results receive over 75% of clicks. Our goal is to get you ranking in the top 3 for your most valuable keywords, which will significantly increase your organic traffic and leads.

Will SEO work for my industry?

SEO works for virtually every industry, from retail stores to professional services. The strategy may differ based on your industry, but the fundamentals remain the same. We've helped businesses across various industries in Greensburg PA improve their search rankings and attract more customers through SEO.
