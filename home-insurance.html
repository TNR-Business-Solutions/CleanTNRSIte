Comprehensive Homeowners Insurance Coverage in Greensburg, Pennsylvania

At TNR Business Solutions, we specialize in providing comprehensive homeowners insurance coverage for families throughout Greensburg, Pennsylvania and the surrounding Westmoreland County area. Our experienced insurance professionals work with over 200 top-rated insurance carriers to find you the best homeowners insurance rates and coverage options that protect your home, belongings, and financial future.

We understand that your home is more than just a building – it's where memories are made, families grow, and dreams are realized. Our comprehensive approach includes everything from dwelling coverage and personal property protection to liability coverage and additional living expenses, ensuring you have the right coverage for your home's unique characteristics and your family's specific needs.

During the consultation process, we focus on understanding your home's value, construction type, location, and your family's lifestyle to recommend the most appropriate coverage options. This includes everything from replacement cost coverage and personal property limits to liability protection and additional endorsements for high-value items.

Homeowners Insurance Coverage Options

Dwelling Coverage

Protects the physical structure of your home from covered perils like fire, wind, hail, and vandalism.

  • Replacement cost coverage
  • Actual cash value options
  • Extended replacement cost
  • Guaranteed replacement cost

Personal Property

Covers your belongings inside your home, including furniture, electronics, clothing, and other personal items.

  • Contents coverage
  • Off-premises coverage
  • Scheduled personal property
  • High-value item coverage

Liability Protection

Protects you if someone is injured on your property or if you accidentally damage someone else's property.

  • Personal liability coverage
  • Medical payments to others
  • Legal defense costs
  • Umbrella policy options

Additional Living Expenses

If your home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered loss, ALE coverage helps pay for temporary housing and living expenses.

  • Hotel and temporary housing
  • Additional food expenses
  • Storage costs for belongings
  • Transportation expenses

Other Structures

Protects detached structures on your property that aren't connected to your main dwelling.

  • Detached garages
  • Sheds and storage buildings
  • Fences and gates
  • Swimming pools and decks

Flood Insurance

Separate flood insurance coverage to protect your home from flood damage, which is not covered by standard homeowners policies.

  • Building coverage
  • Contents coverage
  • NFIP and private options
  • Flood zone considerations

High-Value Home

Specialized coverage for luxury and high-value homes with enhanced protection features and higher coverage limits.

  • Enhanced coverage limits
  • Agreed value coverage
  • Extended replacement cost
  • Concierge claims service

Rental Property

Specialized coverage for rental properties and investment homes with landlord-specific protection.

  • Landlord liability coverage
  • Loss of rental income
  • Property damage coverage
  • Tenant-related risks

Condo Insurance

Specialized coverage for condominium owners with coverage for interior structures and personal belongings.

  • Interior structure coverage
  • Personal property protection
  • Loss assessment coverage
  • Liability protection
