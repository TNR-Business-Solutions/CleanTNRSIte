Business Insurance Coverage Options

General Liability

Essential coverage that protects your business from third-party claims of bodily injury, property damage, and personal injury.

  • Bodily injury protection
  • Property damage coverage
  • Personal and advertising injury
  • Legal defense costs
  • Medical payments

Commercial Property

Protects your business property, equipment, and inventory from covered perils like fire, theft, and vandalism.

  • Building coverage
  • Equipment protection
  • Inventory coverage
  • Business interruption
  • Extra expense coverage

Workers' Compensation

Required by Pennsylvania law for most businesses, provides medical care and wage replacement for work-related injuries.

  • Medical expense coverage
  • Lost wage replacement
  • Disability benefits
  • Rehabilitation services
  • Legal protection

Professional Liability

Protects professionals from claims of errors, omissions, or negligence in providing professional services.

  • Errors and omissions coverage
  • Professional negligence
  • Defense cost coverage
  • Regulatory proceedings
  • Prior acts coverage

Cyber Liability

Essential protection against data breaches, cyber attacks, and technology-related business interruptions.

  • Data breach response
  • Cyber extortion coverage
  • Business interruption
  • Network security liability
  • Regulatory fines

Employment Practices

Protects against claims of wrongful termination, discrimination, harassment, and other employment-related issues.

  • Wrongful termination
  • Discrimination claims
  • Sexual harassment
  • Wage and hour disputes
  • Defense costs

Directors & Officers

Protects company directors and officers from personal liability for management decisions and corporate actions.

  • Management liability
  • Corporate reimbursement
  • Entity coverage
  • Employment practices
  • Fiduciary liability

Product Liability

Essential coverage for manufacturers and retailers protecting against claims from defective products.

  • Product defect claims
  • Manufacturing errors
  • Design defects
  • Warning label issues
  • Recall expenses

Commercial Auto

Coverage for business vehicles, including company cars, delivery trucks, and contractor equipment.

  • Fleet vehicle coverage
  • Commercial liability
  • Hired/non-owned auto
  • Cargo coverage
  • Motor truck cargo
