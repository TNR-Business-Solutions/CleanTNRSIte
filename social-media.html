Frequently Asked Questions About Social Media Management

Which social media platforms should my business be on?

The best platforms depend on your business type and target audience. Facebook is essential for most businesses, Instagram works great for visual businesses, LinkedIn is ideal for B2B services, and Twitter is valuable for real-time engagement. During our consultation, we'll recommend the platforms that make the most sense for your Greensburg business.

How often should I post on social media?

Posting frequency varies by platform and industry, but consistency is key. Generally, we recommend 3-5 posts per week on Facebook, daily posts on Instagram, and 2-3 posts per week on LinkedIn. We'll create a content calendar tailored to your business that maintains optimal posting frequency without overwhelming your audience.

Can I still post on my social media accounts?

Absolutely! We encourage collaboration and can coordinate with your posts. Many clients prefer to handle personal posts while we manage business content, or we can work together on a shared content calendar. Our goal is to create a cohesive social media presence that reflects your brand authentically.

How do you measure social media success?

We track multiple metrics including engagement rates, reach, follower growth, website traffic from social media, and conversions. Our monthly reports show these metrics clearly so you can see your ROI. We focus on metrics that matter to your business goals, whether that's brand awareness, lead generation, or customer engagement.

What's included in your social media management services?

Our comprehensive services include content creation, post scheduling, community management, engagement monitoring, monthly reporting, and strategy development. We also offer paid social media advertising management and influencer outreach. View our packages for detailed service information and pricing.
