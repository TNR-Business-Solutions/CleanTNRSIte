# Fix Meta Verification - Update Old Phone Number

## üî¥ Problem: Meta Verification Using Old Phone Number

If Meta is asking you to verify using an old phone number you no longer have access to, you need to update your phone number or use alternative verification methods.

## ‚úÖ Solution: Update Phone Number or Use Alternative Verification

### Option 1: Update Phone Number in Meta Account Settings (Recommended)

1. **Go to Meta Account Settings**
   - Go to **facebook.com/settings**
   - Or go to **Meta Business Suite ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Personal Information**

2. **Update Phone Number**
   - Click on **"Contact"** or **"Personal Information"**
   - Find your phone number in the list
   - Click **"Remove"** or **"Edit"** next to the old phone number
   - Click **"Add Phone Number"** to add your new phone number
   - Enter your new phone number
   - Verify the new phone number with a code sent via SMS

3. **Set New Phone Number as Primary**
   - After verifying the new phone number, set it as your primary contact method
   - Remove the old phone number if possible
   - Save changes

4. **Retry Verification**
   - Go back to the verification prompt
   - It should now use your new phone number
   - Complete verification with the new number

### Option 2: Use Email Verification Instead

1. **Check if Email Verification is Available**
   - When Meta asks for verification, look for **"Verify with Email"** option
   - Click on **"Use Email Instead"** or **"Verify with Email"**
   - Meta will send a verification code to your email address

2. **Check Your Email**
   - Go to your email inbox (check spam/junk folder too)
   - Look for an email from Meta/Facebook
   - Click the verification link or enter the code

3. **Complete Verification**
   - Enter the verification code from your email
   - Complete the verification process

### Option 3: Use Two-Factor Authentication App

1. **Set Up Authenticator App**
   - Go to **facebook.com/settings/security**
   - Click **"Two-Factor Authentication"**
   - Click **"Use Authentication App"**
   - Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
   - Save the backup codes

2. **Use Authenticator for Verification**
   - When Meta asks for verification, choose **"Use Authentication App"**
   - Enter the code from your authenticator app
   - Complete verification

### Option 4: Contact Meta Support

If none of the above options work, contact Meta Support:

1. **Go to Meta Support**
   - Go to **facebook.com/help**
   - Click **"Get Help"** or **"Contact Support"**
   - Select **"Account Access"** or **"Security and Login"**

2. **Report the Issue**
   - Explain that you no longer have access to the old phone number
   - Provide your current email address
   - Provide proof of identity (driver's license, passport, etc.)
   - Request to update your phone number

3. **Follow Meta's Instructions**
   - Meta support will guide you through the verification process
   - They may ask for additional information to verify your identity
   - Once verified, they can update your phone number

### Option 5: Use Account Recovery

1. **Go to Account Recovery**
   - Go to **facebook.com/login/identify**
   - Enter your email address or username
   - Click **"Search"**

2. **Choose Recovery Method**
   - Select **"No longer have access to these?"**
   - Choose **"Recover your account with help from friends"** (if available)
   - Or choose **"Get more help"**

3. **Follow Recovery Steps**
   - Meta will guide you through account recovery
   - You may need to provide identity verification
   - Once recovered, update your phone number

## üîç Step-by-Step: Update Phone Number in Meta Account

### For Personal Facebook Account:

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Log in to Facebook
   - Click the **down arrow** (‚ñº) in the top right
   - Click **"Settings & Privacy"** ‚Üí **"Settings"**

2. **Update Contact Information**
   - Click **"Personal Information"** in the left sidebar
   - Find **"Contact"** section
   - Click **"Add"** next to phone number (or **"Edit"** if one exists)

3. **Add New Phone Number**
   - Enter your new phone number
   - Choose your country code
   - Click **"Continue"**

4. **Verify New Phone Number**
   - Meta will send a verification code via SMS
   - Enter the code in the verification field
   - Click **"Confirm"**

5. **Remove Old Phone Number**
   - After verifying the new number, go back to **"Personal Information"**
   - Find the old phone number
   - Click **"Remove"** next to it
   - Confirm removal

### For Meta Business Suite:

1. **Go to Business Settings**
   - Go to **business.facebook.com**
   - Click **"Settings"** in the left sidebar
   - Click **"Business Info"**

2. **Update Contact Information**
   - Find **"Contact Information"** section
   - Click **"Edit"** next to phone number
   - Add your new phone number
   - Verify with code sent via SMS

3. **Update Personal Information**
   - Go to **"People"** in the left sidebar
   - Find your name
   - Click **"Edit"** next to contact information
   - Update phone number
   - Verify with code
