Auto Insurance Coverage Options

Liability Coverage

Required by Pennsylvania law, liability coverage protects you financially if you cause an accident that injures others or damages their property.

  • Bodily injury liability
  • Property damage liability
  • Pennsylvania minimum requirements

Comprehensive & Collision

Full coverage that protects your vehicle against damage from accidents, theft, vandalism, weather, and other covered perils.

  • Collision coverage for accidents
  • Comprehensive for theft/weather
  • Flexible deductible options

Additional Protection

Extra coverage options to ensure you're fully protected on Pennsylvania roads and beyond.

  • Uninsured/underinsured motorist
  • Personal injury protection (PIP)
  • Rental car reimbursement
  • Roadside assistance

Personal Injury Protection

Coverage for medical expenses and lost wages regardless of who's at fault in an accident.

  • Medical expense coverage
  • Lost wage replacement
  • Essential services coverage

Uninsured Motorist

Protection when you're involved in an accident with an uninsured or underinsured driver.

  • Bodily injury coverage
  • Property damage coverage
  • Hit-and-run protection

Roadside Assistance

24/7 emergency assistance for breakdowns, flat tires, lockouts, and other roadside emergencies.

  • Towing services
  • Jump start assistance
  • Lockout service
  • Flat tire changes

Classic Car Insurance

Specialized coverage for classic, antique, and collector vehicles with agreed value protection.

  • Agreed value coverage
  • Spare parts coverage
  • Show car coverage

High-Value Vehicle

Specialized coverage for luxury and high-value vehicles with enhanced protection features.

  • Enhanced coverage limits
  • Original equipment parts
  • Diminished value protection

Commercial Auto

Business vehicle coverage for company cars, delivery vehicles, and commercial transportation.

  • Business use coverage
  • Hired vehicle coverage
  • Non-owned vehicle coverage
