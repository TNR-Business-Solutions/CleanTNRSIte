Comprehensive Umbrella Insurance Coverage in Greensburg, Pennsylvania

At TNR Business Solutions, we specialize in providing comprehensive umbrella insurance coverage for individuals and businesses throughout Greensburg, Pennsylvania and the surrounding Westmoreland County area. Our experienced insurance professionals work with over 200 top-rated insurance carriers to find you the best umbrella insurance rates and coverage options that provide additional protection beyond your primary policies.

We understand that your primary insurance policies have coverage limits, and a single lawsuit or major claim could exceed those limits, leaving your personal assets vulnerable. Our comprehensive approach includes everything from personal umbrella policies to commercial excess liability coverage, ensuring you have the right additional protection for your unique situation and risk profile.

During the consultation process, we focus on understanding your current insurance coverage, assets, and potential liability exposure to recommend the most appropriate umbrella coverage options. This includes everything from coverage limits and policy terms to additional endorsements and specialized protection for high-net-worth individuals and businesses.

Umbrella Insurance Coverage Options

Personal Umbrella

Additional liability protection for individuals and families beyond your auto and home insurance limits.

  • Excess liability coverage
  • Worldwide protection
  • Defense cost coverage
  • Personal injury protection

Commercial Umbrella

Additional liability protection for businesses beyond your general liability and commercial auto limits.

  • Excess general liability
  • Employment practices coverage
  • Directors & officers protection
  • Professional liability excess

High-Value Umbrella

Specialized umbrella coverage for high-net-worth individuals with enhanced protection features.

  • Higher coverage limits
  • Uninsured motorist excess
  • Defamation coverage
  • Landlord liability excess

Excess Liability

Additional coverage that kicks in after your primary policies reach their limits.

  • Drop-down coverage
  • Gap coverage options
  • Self-insured retention
  • Aggregate limits

Professional Excess

Additional protection for professionals beyond their errors and omissions coverage.

  • Medical malpractice excess
  • Legal malpractice excess
  • Architect/engineer excess
  • Technology errors excess

Excess Auto

Additional auto liability protection beyond your personal or commercial auto limits.

  • Bodily injury excess
  • Property damage excess
  • Uninsured motorist excess
  • Hired/non-owned excess
