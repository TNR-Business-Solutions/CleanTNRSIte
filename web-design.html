Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project, but most websites are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple brochure sites can be ready in as little as 1-2 weeks, while more complex e-commerce sites may take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we design are fully responsive and mobile-optimized. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile devices, we ensure your site looks great and functions perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile-friendliness is also crucial for Google rankings.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive website maintenance packages for Greensburg PA businesses. Services include security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, backups, and technical support. Monthly maintenance plans ensure your website stays secure, fast, and up-to-date.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes! We build websites using content management systems that allow you to easily update text, images, and other content without technical knowledge. We provide training during the handoff process so you can manage your site confidently. We're also available for updates if you prefer.

What's included in your web design packages?

Our packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content management system setup, basic training, and initial launch support. Additional services like e-commerce functionality, advanced features, and ongoing maintenance can be added to any package. View our packages for detailed information.
